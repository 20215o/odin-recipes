Rice & Beans

A classic and hearty dish loved around the world. Rice and beans are nutritious, filling, and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 cup beans
  • 1 onion
  • cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsps oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onion and garlic.
  2. Add rice and stir for 2 minutes.
  3. Add 2 cups of water and cook until rice is almost tender.
  4. Mix in beans, season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer until rice is fully cooked.

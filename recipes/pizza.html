Homemade Pizza

Homemade pizza that’s crispy, cheesy, and customizable with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • ½ cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Pepperoni or any topping of choice
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  3. Add cheese and toppings.
  4. Drizzle lightly with olive oil.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes until crust is golden

Fresh Salad

A light and refreshing salad that’s perfect as a side dish or healthy meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups lettuce (chopped)
  • 1 cucumber (sliced)
  • 1 tomato (diced)
  • ½ onion (sliced thin)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Place lettuce, cucumber, tomato, and onion in a bowl.
  2. In a small cup, mix olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour dressing over vegetables.
  4. Toss gently to combine.
  5. Serve immediately.
